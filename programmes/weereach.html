WeeReach

Our local service arm, WeeReach was started in August 2018 because students wanted ways to give back to society locally.

We are currently engaging more than 50 volunteers in various local volunteer projects.

We have weekly volunteer programmes with Jamiyah Children's Home and Jamiyah Home for the Aged, and we are also partnering with Beyond Social Services for various ad hoc projects and a social media campaign.

Our Partners

2018
Jamiyah Home for the Aged

Jamiyah Home for the Aged (Darul Takrim) was established in 1996 as an eldercare and destitute welfare facility by the then-Ministry of Community Development with the appointment of the Muslim Missionary Society Singapore as the managing agent.

Our volunteer activities include befriending the residents and organising activities such as game shows, performances, arts and crafts and bingo, to allow residents to experience things they otherwise would not be able to. We also organise celebrations for different festivities like Chinese New Year and Hari Raya.

Jamiyah Children's Home

Jamiyah Children's Home (Darul Ma'wa) is a Voluntary Welfare Organisation (VWO) established by Jamiyah Singapore (Muslim Missionary Society Singapore) which is a full member of the National Council of Social Services in Singapore.

Our volunteers plan elaborate tuition and creative creative writing classes to help residents with their school work. We also organise games and day camps for the residents to relax and enjoy during their school holidays.

Beyond Social Services

Beyond Social Services is a non-profit charity dedicated to helping children and youths from less privileged backgrounds break away from the poverty cycle. They provide guidance, care and resources that enable families and communities to keep their young people in school and out of trouble.

Our team is currently working closely with Beyond Social Services to plan a social media campaign to raise awareness of the issue poverty in Singapore. We also provide manpower for various ad hoc activities and fundraisers that they need assistance with.
