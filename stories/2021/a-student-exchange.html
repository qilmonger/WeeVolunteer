Learning In Laos: A Student Exchange With Zack And Khamhuk

by Ezekiel L S Sen, Irene Cheong and Song Yue Rong
Published 27th May 2022

Zack comes from India, and Khamhuk hails from Laos, a 22-hour flight away.

While the duo have never met before, they've both undertaken unconventional pathways in their pursuit of higher education.
